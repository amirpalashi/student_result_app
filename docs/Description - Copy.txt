# Student Result Management System

**Student Result Management System** is a powerful, secure, and user-friendly academic management application designed for **Schools, Colleges, Madrasahs, Coaching Centers, and Educational Institutions**. The app helps teachers, administrators, and educational organizations efficiently manage student information, academic records, examination results, and professional digital marksheets from a single platform.

Built with modern technology and an intuitive interface, the application simplifies daily academic tasks while ensuring accuracy, reliability, and ease of use.

---

## Key Features

### Student Management

* Add, edit, update, and manage student information
* Organize student records by academic session, class, and group
* Quick student search using Student ID

### Subject Management

* Create and manage subjects for different classes
* Configure Full Marks and Pass Marks
* Flexible subject organization

### Result Entry

* Simple and fast marks entry
* Automatic validation of marks
* Easy editing and updating of results

### Automatic GPA & Grade Calculation

* Instant GPA calculation
* Automatic grade generation
* Accurate academic result processing

### Professional Digital Marksheet

* Clean and professional marksheet design
* Easy-to-read subject-wise results
* Attractive result summary
* Pass/Fail status
* Total Marks
* Average Marks
* Final Grade
* GPA

### PDF Export & Printing

* Generate high-quality PDF marksheets
* Print professional reports
* Share marksheets instantly
* Suitable for academic record keeping

### School Information Management

* Customize institution information
* School name
* Address
* EIIN
* Contact information
* Institution logo support

### Secure Offline Database

* Uses SQLite local database
* Works without an internet connection
* Fast performance
* Reliable local data storage

## Privacy & Security

Your data is important.

Student Result Management System stores academic information locally on your device using a secure SQLite database. The application does not automatically upload your student records or examination data to external servers.

PDF generation, printing, and file sharing are performed only when initiated by the user.
